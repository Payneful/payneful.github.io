Overview

Purpose

To help others enjoy fishing as much as I do and to provide the necessary information needed to enjoy catching a lot of fish. I also want people to get excited about trying new lures or even visiting somewhere new, but knowing what fish you can catch and what lures have worked best there. It will also allow others to put in different places that may be good for fishing and what lures they used to catch what fish so that others can also spread the word.

Audience

Anyone who is interested in learning more about different ways to fish. I try to help people weather they are just old enough to go fishing or if they have fished their whole life and just need a little help learning how to fish for new types of fish.

Content

Home page

We are pro fishing and we want to help every have an enjoyable time on the water fishing, and to learn more about how to not get skunked when fishing. When you go fishing at a new place you don't want to leave empty handed. A good place to look would be in our locations section that allows you to see what kind of fish are located where. After that you can click on the different fish that are shown to be brought to a page that will tell you exactly what lure you need to hammer those fish.

Images for the Home page

Lures and Species

Here is a list of different species of fish. You are able to click on any of the fish to see what kind of lures may be good to catch them. These are lures that have either been used by me or someone I know, or by someone who has supported the website and has put in their lucky lure they used.

Locations

Here are some pictures of places that you can click on. Each will populate different known bodies of water that are in the area. You can access each location shown and it will bring you to a list of different fish in that location. You can click on the fish to get specific information on them.
